About Carley Oates

Carley is a mother, student midwife (NHS), yoga teacher, antenatal teacher and is committed to sharing her passion for the life enhancing experiences of pregnancy, childbirth and parenting. For over 20 years she has been studying health and well being, sparked by her fascination of human potential and evolution in action.

Her current training as an NHS midwife has prompted her to offer antenatal classes for couples which respond to the needs of individual women and their birth/parenting partners. Carley has been praised by her mentors for her professionalism, excellent communication skills and 'with women' understanding of midwifery.

With sensitive and nurturing classes, Carley has supported hundreds of women to enjoy healthy pregnancies and positive birth experiences. Carley is currently offering weekly pregnancy yoga classes for women and monthly antenatal classes for couples.

Testimonials

Every woman has such a unique experience of pregnancy and birth which can have a profound impact on her experience of mothering. Here is just some of the recent feedback Carley has received from women she has worked with:

A mother who had a traumatic birth with her first baby……

“thank you for the wonderful classes and your support through pregnancy. It was fantastic to have time to focus on myself and the baby and the classes were really helpful… it was such a different experience to having my first, so calm but incredibly precious. I felt awake and able to bond and have wonderful skin-to-skin straight after he was born. He latched on and is doing really well breastfeeding.”

A mother who attended yoga classes with Carley before she was pregnant and during both pregnancies…

“So thank you for all the yoga and breathing techniques you have taught me over the years, they really work!”

A mother whose labour was induced in her first pregnancy…

“Thanks for all your support… It turned out that my body did understand how to go into labour naturally!!!”

A mother on birthing her first baby…

“Thanks for your yoga teaching and such lovely classes. The breathing techniques really helped, the midwives were amazed at how calm I was throughout the labour.”

A mother who was due to have her labour induced for medical reasons, but she began labour naturally and gave birth quickly…

”Breathing & mindfulness definitely helped me through the pain! I will be continuing with the breathing techniques while breastfeeding and I’m going to keep doing the Yoga Nidra to help me relax.”

A couple who Carley supported with birth preparation plans for a homebirth…

”Thanks for the really nice encouragement you gave us, we both really appreciated it. The birth was quick, I breathed loudly through the intense contractions. Natural 3rd stage and no stitching ☺”

A mother who gave birth to her baby weighing 11lb14…

“the yoga really helped me stay focused and controlled”

A mother who birthed her baby in hospital under consultant-led care…

“I would like to thank you for everything we did in the yoga classes it was really useful during labour and I spent the majority of my time on hands and knees and using the ball. It helped with the contractions and moving things along.”

A mother who birthed her first baby at home…
