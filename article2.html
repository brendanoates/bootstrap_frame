Antenatal Services April 2015

Antenatal Classes for Couples

Monthly classes for pregnant women and their birth partners, ideal for couples from 5 months of pregnancy onwards.

The following dates are available to book, Saturday afternoons, 2-5pm:

  • 23rd May
  • 20th June
  • 18th July

Classes are held with The Natural Pregnancy Partnership at Yanley Court, Long Ashton BS41 9LB


Classes cover:

  • How to make the most out of pregnancy
  • Enjoying a healthy pregnancy as a couple
  • Preparing for positive and empowering childbirth
  • Life with a newborn baby
The classes promote physical and emotional well-being through:
  • Education; the latest evidence-based information to help you know your options and make the right choices for you and your family. Negotiating your needs within the maternity health care system.
  • Skills and Techniques; relaxation, massage, self-hypnosis/concentration exercises, yoga exercises and breathing practices, effectively communicating your preferences to care providers.
  • Discussion; a safe supportive group to voice the questions which are important to you, share ideas and find solutions to suit you individually and as a couple.

The investment is £40 per couple for each session. Discount price of £100 for booking 3 sessions.

For further details, contact Carley

Yoga For Pregnancy Classes

Thursday evenings 7.30pm – 9.00pm

Weekly classes held with The Natural Pregnancy Partnership at Yanley Court, Long Ashton BS41 9LB

£45 for 5 classes

  • Nourish yourself & your baby
  • Increase confidence
  • Relax and restore energy
  • Relieve pain & discomfort in a safe, friendly and supportive environment

Learn yoga techniques which aid the ability to manage and accept the physical, emotional and energetic changes during pregnancy and the journey towards motherhood; connecting you with your unborn child and with the joy of nurturing the new life within.

The classes will support you during your pregnancy and as you prepare for the birth of your baby. You can start the classes at any time during your pregnancy, but most women begin during the second trimester (12 weeks onwards). You are welcome to continue attending the classes throughout your pregnancy as you deepen your understanding of how yoga can benefit you and your baby and to truely embody the techniques you learn.

For further details, contact Carley

About Carley Oates

Carley is a mother, student midwife (NHS), yoga teacher, antenatal teacher and is committed to sharing her passion for the life enhancing experiences of pregnancy, childbirth and parenting. For over 20 years she has been studying health and well being, sparked by her fascination of human potential and evolution in action.

Her current training as an NHS midwife has prompted her to offer antenatal classes for couples which respond to the needs of individual women and their birth/parenting partners. Carley has been praised by her mentors for her professionalism, excellent communication skills and 'with women' understanding of midwifery.

With sensitive and nurturing classes, Carley has supported hundreds of women to enjoy healthy pregnancies and positive birth experiences. Carley is currently offering weekly pregnancy yoga classes for women and monthly antenatal classes for couples.
